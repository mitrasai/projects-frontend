I Love Food

Sick and tired of the same boring food everyday? On the occasion of the World Food Day, why do not you try something different? And talking about different, why not indulge in some really delicious dishes from Hyderabad. From biryani to kebabs to sweet dishes, the royal Hyderabadi cuisine has it all.


List Of Products

Mirchi ka Salan

Having a slow day? Let the hot-pungent taste of Mirchi ka Salan give you the kick you so badly crave. The perfect balance in the dish comes from the coconut paste that is added for a unique, refreshing flavour. This Hyderabadi cuisine will make you come back for more!

Hyderabadi Biryani

You cannot talk about biryanis without mentioning the Hyderabadi Biryani. Cooked with either mutton or chicken, and laced with rich ingredients, this recipe is the stairway to heaven. Believe it or not, this dish is such a favourite that people do not mind having it for anything
